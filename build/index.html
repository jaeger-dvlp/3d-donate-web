We are expanding our organization, which we started in 2018, in a way that will benefit the whole world. We aim to touch the lives of thousands of people and meet their needs with this completely free work. As 3D Donate, our goal is education, health, child development, social development, nature and animals all over the world. to provide support such as protection.

3DDonate.com, Occupational therapist İsmail İşler and Emre Akıncı PhD. was organized by Zaxe 3D with the support of Zaxe Z3 3D printers to produce products for all creatures in need, completely free of charge.

Our products are free of charge, including production and shipping services. It is produced with PLA or ABS filament depending on the need.
